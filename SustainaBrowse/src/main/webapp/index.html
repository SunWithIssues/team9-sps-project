About SustainaBrowse

SustainaBrowse is a Chrome extension to help users evaluate the sustainability of their purchases and get tips on how to reduce their carbon footprint

How It Works

SustainaBrowse works by providing friendly reminders on ways users can reduce their waste and improve their ethical consumption and use of goods! Get started now with just a click of a button.
